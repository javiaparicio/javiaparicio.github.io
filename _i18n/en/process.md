<h2>1. Before the session</h2>

<p>Before the shoot we talk by email or message to understand:</p>
<ul>
<li>What you do</li>
<li>What you need the photos for</li>
<li>Where you will use them (LinkedIn, website, social media, press, etc.)</li>
</ul>

<p>That way I can align the session with your profile and the image you want to convey.</p>

<hr class="content-divider">
<h2>2. On the day of the session</h2>

<p>The session takes place in my Bern studio or at a location in the city.</p>

<p>During the session:</p>
<ul>
<li>I guide you on posture, expression and presence</li>
<li>I help you relax and feel comfortable in front of the camera</li>
<li>We adapt pace and style to your needs</li>
</ul>

<p>You do not need to know how to pose. My job is to lead the session so the result looks natural and professional.</p>

<hr class="content-divider">
<h2>3. Selection and editing</h2>

<p>After the session:</p>
<ul>
<li>I select the best photos</li>
<li>I edit them for a clean, natural and consistent style</li>
<li>I prepare versions ready for web and social media</li>
</ul>

<hr class="content-divider">
<h2>4. Delivery</h2>

<p>You receive:</p>
<ul>
<li>High-resolution images</li>
<li>Optimised versions for LinkedIn, website and social media</li>
<li>Photos you can use directly in your communication</li>
</ul>

<p>Delivery is digital.</p>
