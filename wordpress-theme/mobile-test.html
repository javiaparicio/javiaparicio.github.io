Mobile Menu Test

Resize your browser window to test the mobile menu functionality.

On mobile devices (768px and below), you should see a hamburger menu (☰) in the top-right corner.

Click the hamburger menu to open/close the sidebar.

Test Instructions:

  1. Resize your browser window to be narrow (less than 768px wide)
  2. Look for the hamburger menu (☰) in the top-right corner
  3. Click the hamburger menu to open the sidebar
  4. Click outside the sidebar or on a menu link to close it
  5. Test on different screen sizes
